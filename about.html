About Space for Data Scientists

一個簡單紀錄筆記的地方，希望能透過分享自己所學/經歷來讓下個想做一樣事情的人飛得更快。


主要會 cover 資料科學, Python, 機器學習以及可視化技巧，歡迎各種回饋

A simple blog trying to keep record of what I have learnt as a data scientist / software engineer. By sharing my notes/thoughts here, I hope you will benefit from it and that I'm eager to learn more from you too.
